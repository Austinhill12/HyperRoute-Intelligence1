Privacy Policy

How HyperRoute Intelligence handles operational data.

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Information Collected

The platform may store account, company, user, driver, vehicle, load, customer, invoice, document, maintenance, compliance, support, and activity log information.

How Data Is Used

Data is used to operate the platform, provide customer support, maintain security, generate reports, and improve business workflows for transportation operations.

Company Separation

Customer data is intended to be separated by company workspace so users only access records connected to their authorized company or platform role.

Third-Party Services

The platform may rely on third-party services for authentication, database hosting, file storage, email, analytics, payment processing, and infrastructure.

Data Retention

Operational data may be retained while an account is active and for a reasonable period afterward for security, billing, backup, or legal reasons.

Contact

Privacy or account data requests should be submitted through the Support page.
